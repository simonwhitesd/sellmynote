    What does note means in real estate?

    A mortgage note is a document signed by the borrower when they secure a loan. It details the terms of the loan, such as the amount borrowed, interest rate, repayment plan, and any other pertinent information. The bank that finances the loan holds the mortgage on the property until the loan is repaid in full.

This agreement is usually included in the deed of purchase and sale.
